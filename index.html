      Our Manifesto

      We don't list tools.
      We curate them.

      Forma exists because finding the right AI tool shouldn't take three hours of research and a dozen browser tabs.

      01 — The Problem

      The AI tool landscape is overwhelming

      Every week, dozens of new AI tools launch with bold promises. Most are noise. Some are genuinely useful. A handful are transformative. But they all look the same in a list.

      We started Forma because we were tired of spending hours evaluating tools that turned out to be mediocre, expensive, or simply not built for real professional use. There had to be a better way.

      02 — Our Approach

      Curation is an opinion, not an algorithm

      Every tool in Forma has been evaluated against the same criteria. We don't accept sponsored placements. We don't rank by affiliate revenue. We rank by usefulness.

      • Does it work reliably? We only list tools that are stable and actively maintained.
      • Is the value clear? If we can't explain what it does in one sentence, it doesn't belong here.
      • Is the pricing honest? We flag hidden costs, aggressive upsells, and bait-and-switch free tiers.
      • Does it respect the user? No dark patterns. No data harvesting fine print. No manipulative design.
      03 — By the Numbers

      Small, intentional, growing

      We'd rather have 24 tools you can trust than 2,400 tools that dilute the signal. Quality over quantity, always.

      04 — Our Promise

      What we commit to, every week

      Forma is updated every week. New tools are added only when they genuinely earn their place. Existing tools are removed if they decline in quality or close down.

      The Forma Standard

      If a tool isn't good enough that we'd personally recommend it to a friend, it doesn't go in the directory. That's the only filter that matters.
